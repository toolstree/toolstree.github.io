About Bharat Ghar Docs

Bharat Ghar Docs (https://toolstree.github.io/Bharat-Ghar/) is a free initiative started in Thane, Maharashtra to help Indian tenants and housing societies generate common documents without paying Rs 50-100 per receipt to paid tools.

We provide HRA Rent Receipts for tax exemption under Section 80GG, 11-month Rent Agreements, Maintenance Receipts, NOC for Passport, Tenant Police Verification NOC, and Rent Increment Letters. All formats are based on commonly accepted society formats in Maharashtra and India.

Why Free? Paywall models get copied in one day. Free model with Google AdSense ensures lakhs of families get access and we earn sustainably via ads.

We do not store any user data. Everything runs in browser.

Built by Toolstree - an indie tools initiative.
